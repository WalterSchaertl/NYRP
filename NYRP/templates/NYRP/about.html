About New York Regents Prep

This is a website aiming to help New York students with studying for the end of year state tests. From here you are able to select real regents exam questions from previous years to practice. Questions can be chosen either by the year they appeared or by the topic/unit they belong to.

There are unlimited attempts allowed per question, giving you immediate feedback. After finishing all the questions, or ending the test early if you need a break, a breakdown of your performance is shown. It displays your overall results, as well as a break down of whatever questions you got wrong by unit to assist in additional targeted studying.

If you wish to see the source code, it's hosted on Github here.

How the grading works:

To determine the percentage of questions scored as “correct”, only questions answered on the first attempt are counted. Questions that were skipped, or answered after the first try, are considered incorrect. If you’re using this as a practice and skipped all the units you haven’t covered yet, then the results may be off.
